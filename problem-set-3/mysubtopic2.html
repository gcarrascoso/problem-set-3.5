About Us

Who are we?

Our team is a nonprofit organization, devoted to entertaining and educating our viewers on Fortnite Emotes and other Fortnite related subject. Our staff is operated by many fabulous employees and we all play a role in making this company run smoothly. Our company was fouded in 20016 and has then been growing exponentionally, reaching out to over 50 thousand people over the past 6 months.

Where are we from?

Our company was originally based in downtown Manhattan, but we have then since grown and expanded our locations internationally to the UK, Japan, and Canada.
