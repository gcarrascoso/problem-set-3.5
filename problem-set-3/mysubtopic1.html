Emote Rankings

8. Flapper

This classic emote is based off of the famous "Charleston Dance" which would be performed by "flappers" (hence the name) in the 1920s. It is an emote people of any age can appreicate.

7. Wiggle

This emote is quite a groovy one, with smooth and fluid motions. The goofiness of this dance fits in perfectly with the fun nature of the game.

6. Squat Kick

Based off the Russian folk dance, this emote takes real balance and coordination to perform in real life. It is definitely one that would impress many when executed making for a very memorable emote.

5. Take The L

A fan favorite, the Take The L emote is possibly the most direspectful emote to perform after beating your opponent. Seeing the dance after loosing can make on very salty.

4. Best Mates

Based off of a viral vine by the social media influencer, Marlon Webb, Best Mates is a very fun dance in fortnite and in real life. It makes you want to grab a group of pals and dance in the streets.

3. Orance Justice

Popularized by figures such as Roy Purdy, Orange Justice can make anyone crack up when seeing being performed in public. It perfectly blends comedic actions, and technical dance skill. It is also a popular emote to do amongst younger kids.

2. Electro Shuffle

This emote has to be one of if not the most technically challenging. Based off of a youtube video called 'Bad and Boujee Shuffle Shapes,' the dance includes very fancy footwork for a very cool emote.

1. Defaault!

Number 1 on our list just had to be the default emote. It is the first one anyone gets in the game and is the most iconic. the dance is from the television series Scrubs, and the background music that comes with the emote makes anyone want to attempt the dance! Overall, this emote is by far the greatest.
